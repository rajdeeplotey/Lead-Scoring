### Interpretation in Lead Scoring Context
- **Accuracy**: Overall how well the model classifies leads as Hot/Warm/Cold
- **Precision**: When the model predicts a lead as "Hot", how often is it actually Hot?
- **Recall**: Of all actual Hot leads, how many did the model correctly identify?
- **F1 Score**: Balance between precision and recall - important for sales teams who need both accurate identification and comprehensive coverage

### Note on Training Data
This classifier is trained on labels generated by the rule-based scoring engine, NOT on actual conversion/sales outcome data. 
The metrics above reflect how well the ML model learned to replicate the rule-based logic, not real-world predictive performance.
